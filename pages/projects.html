Poultry feeding at Kanzenze Farm

APTC Poultry Farm is a new project opened at Kanzenze, Bugesera District.

This project is owned by APTC since 31/10/2019.

It counts around 6,000 laying chickens.

This farm is owning five walls with the size estimated at 20Ã—40m each, and one big store which contains the eggs reception room, office, different materials and equipment for poultry farming.

Only three of five walls contain chickens, while the remaining are empty.

The farm has capacity to accommodate around 20,000.

Objectives

Diversification of APTC production (meat & eggs) to enhance food security in terms of proteins.
