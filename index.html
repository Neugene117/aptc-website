About Us

APTC Ltd is an investment Holding Trust Company whose offices are located in heart of Kigali City. It was legally registered under registration code No 1034544201 in accordance with the Rwanda company law on the 24 day of October 2014.

APTC has invested in a wide range of business activities in three subsidiary companies and two stand alone projects as follows; Agro Processing Industries (API) Ltd, Rugari Meat Processing Ltd, Nyanza Milk Industries Ltd, Fertilizer and Seed Distribution...
